The Drain Gain: An investigation into how colonial drain counteracted the fall in the rate of profit

India's colonial history has received substantial scholarly attention, and the drain of wealth has particularly attracted considerable focus. However, the emphasis has often remained on the drain of wealth as a cause of India's underdevelopment. This study shifts the lens to examine how this drain from India to Britain played a crucial role in Britain's economic development.
